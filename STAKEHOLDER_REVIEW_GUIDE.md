# MangaDrama Prototype - Stakeholder Review Guide

## Review Objective
Please review this prototype as a **product structure and UX direction prototype**, not as a final production UI.

The key question is:
> Does the product flow clearly support AI manga drama creation, Douyin launch material generation, pricing conversion, and personal-center retention?

## Suggested Review Order

1. `index.html` — Does the homepage clearly explain the product and convert users?
2. `features.html` — Are the product capabilities detailed enough?
3. `templates.html` — Do the template categories support Chinese market demand?
4. `showcase.html` — Do the cases explain value and proof?
5. `pricing.html` — Is the subscription + 积分 + project package logic clear?
6. `login.html` — Is the onboarding path simple enough?
7. `account.html` — Does the personal center support membership, 积分, assets, orders and authorization?
8. `studio.html` — Does the workspace feel like a useful production dashboard?
9. `wizard.html` — Is the script-to-series flow understandable?
10. `launch-kit.html` — Does the Douyin launch kit clearly differentiate the product?

## What to Review

### Must-review items
- Product positioning
- Page structure
- Conversion path
- Feature completeness
- 价格页 logic
- Personal center modules
- Douyin launch kit differentiation

### Do not over-review yet
- Individual AI image text accuracy
- Final icon style
- Final animation details
- Exact production-ready image quality

## Feedback Format

Recommended feedback format:

| Page | Issue | Severity | Suggested Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| index.html | Example: CTA unclear | High / Medium / Low | Example: Change CTA wording |

## Priority Rules

High priority:
- Blocks user understanding
- Blocks conversion
- Confuses product positioning
- Breaks key navigation or flow

Medium priority:
- Content hierarchy unclear
- Module order could improve
- Feature wording needs refinement

Low priority:
- Decorative image preference
- Minor spacing / icon details
- Future animation ideas
